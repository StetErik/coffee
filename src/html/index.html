How we roast our coffee:

New Refining Group is located in Kaliningrad and has its own warehouse and representative office in Moscow. The plant has been operating in the market of freshly roasted coffee and instant products for over 15 years. Factory has German Probat coffee roasting equipment and Italian agglomerators for the production of soluble products.

Coffee comes in containers directly from plantations around the world. The company has long-term product supply contracts. Each batch goes through strict inspection at the factory to check grains. The green beans are roasted, degassed and immediately sent to customers. Our roasters have completed Education in Russia and abroad. We have our own approach to each batch of grain.
